# story_vault.md — first-hand stories for GEMBA Industrial blog

Same rules as the other sites. The auto-blogger will only integrate a
first-person anecdote if a story here matches the weekly candidate topic.
Otherwise, byline is "GEMBA Industrial crew" and the post stays in
third-person ("contractors typically…") or cites public incident reports.

**Confidentiality rule**: never include details that could identify a client,
a named individual, an exact date tied to a known incident, or specific
rate / contract values. Anonymize role titles.

## Template

```markdown
### <short slug> — <one-line summary>

Tags: <tag1, tag2>
Project type: <catalyst changeout | reactor inspection | turnaround etc.>
Refinery (public-allowed or anonymized): <Neste Porvoo | "a Gulf Coast refinery" | ...>
Approx date: <YYYY-MM or year only>

The story (150–300 words, first person, honest, NO client confidentials):
<your text>
```
